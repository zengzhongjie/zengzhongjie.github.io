Zhongjie Zeng 曾仲杰

2013 年获广东工业大学硕士学位。曾负责 DAU 超千万级产品的在线推荐系统、计算广告系统以及内容风控算法研发，业务涵盖信息流、短视频和社交应用。

目前在广东工业大学信息工程学院攻读学术博士（方向：知识工程、智能决策）。同时担任温氏集团算法技术架构和管理工作，主导数据产品研发，包括智能化生产风险管理、生产经营智能决策等。

I obtained a Master's degree from Guangdong University of Technology in 2013. I previously led the R&D of online recommendation systems, computational advertising systems, and content risk control algorithms for products with a DAU of over 10 million, spanning information feeds, short videos, and social applications.

Currently, I am pursuing an Academic Doctorate at the School of Information Engineering, Guangdong University of Technology (specializing in Knowledge Engineering and Intelligent Decision-making). Concurrently, I serve in algorithm technology architecture and management at Wens Group, overseeing data product development such as intelligent production risk management and intelligent decision-making for operations.
